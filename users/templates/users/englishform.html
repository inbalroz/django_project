Informed Consent Form

Title of Research: Bidding on paper reviews

Principle Investigator: Reshef Meir, IE&M, Technion
Dear Participant,

The study is about: Bidding on papers for peer review

You will be asked to: log in to a designated website, and mark a set of papers to
“review" by following instructions. You will not be required to read or review these
papers.

Risks and benefits: there are no risks involved.
Compensation:

When logging in, you will receive a list of keywords, and information on available
papers. Your payment will be determined by the set of papers you will be allocated
(which may not coincide with the papers you mark), and how many keywords they
contain.

Your answers will be confidential: The records of this study will be kept private. In
any sort of report we make public we will not include any information that will make
it possible to identify you without your explicit consent. Research records will be
kept in a locked file; only the researchers will have access to the records.

Taking part is voluntary: Taking part in this study is completely voluntary. If you
decide to take part, you are free to withdraw at any time. You are free to perform all
or some or none of the tasks you will be assigned. However full payment requires
successful completion of tasks.

If you have questions: you may contact Reshef Meir: reshefm@ie.technion.ac.il

Statement of Consent: I have read the above information, and have received
answers to any questions I asked. I consent to take part in the study.
